Getting Started with Domains

New to the domain name game? Check out our steps below for getting started with obtaining a domain name and setting up your website!

1

Brainstorm and Come Prepared

Have a computer, credit card, and some domain name ideas in mind. Be aware that the domain name you want to purchase may already be taken, so it is helpful to have some alternative choices.

2

Search for Your Domain

No Pain Domains offers visitors the option to check the availability on a domain name. While there are many suffixes (also known as top level domains) to choose from, such as .com, .net, or .org, most people select a domain name with the .com suffix. To learn more about the different suffixes, visit our Resource Center.

3

Purchase Your Domain Name

Once you find a domain name you would like to purhcase, visit Namecheap.com to complete your transaction. During your transaction, avoid adding unnecessary add-ons to your purchase (such as WhoIsGuard).

4

Connect to a Hosting Service

After you purchase your domain name, you will want to connect it to a web hosting service (these make your website accessible over the Internet). Common web hosting services include Namecheap, Bluehost, and HostGator.
